WORK PROCESS

Scrum is an agile project management framework designed to enhance collaboration, flexibility, and productivity in complex development projects. It emphasizes iterative progress, continuous feedback, and a self-organizing, cross-functional team structure. Through short, time-boxed work cycles called sprints, Scrum enables teams to adapt to changing requirements and deliver valuable, high-quality products efficiently. The framework promotes transparency, accountability, and regular inspection and adaptation, making it a popular choice for software development and other industries.
